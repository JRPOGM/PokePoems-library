Brain and Brawn

Is the reward in the end not worth the effort put in?
What is your knowledge worth?
What are your muscles for?

You have absorbed. You have dieted.
You have grown, in mass and capacity.
You have shed tears and sweat.
You have survived tears and swears.

Has the end result been beaten into shape?
Or has it beaten the odds?
