A Message from the Obtuse Office

How much of it do you believe,
The farce played at your feet?
At what point do your eyes leave?
When does your attention deplete?
What would have convinced you to stay,
I couldn’t feign to assume.
If I gave a damn then maybe I’d pray,
For you to be the one I still groom.
I put in the effort to convince you.
I got so many of you to comply.
But now you really listen to what I spew,
And now it’s me you decry.
I won’t bother shedding a tear.
I don’t plan on leaving here.

Mr. Mime, Secret 7 of 16
