Language Barrier

Man created signals, not just signs
As means of expression down to even the single gesture

The cardinal tilts of the head convey sentences
Fingers, plural or individual, form words with little effort
The twist of the body in a crowd writes paragraphs to watchful eyes
And those same eyes change sizes to pour out their hearts

Sign language slang
A language anyone can speak
