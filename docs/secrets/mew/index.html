God is All, God is Not

Created all
Knows all
Does all
Is all
One would almost believe God omnipotent

Almost

If God is all, He is but a shallow imitation of any one thing
If God does all, He chooses to be as evil as he could be good
If God knows all, He cannot feign that choice has any power or place
If God created all, He must have given pain and suffering for nothing but his own amusement

If man could ever hope to be as aspiring,
Yet they can rest knowing they will never be as cruel,
Who does that make the lesser?
