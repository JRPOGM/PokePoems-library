Warm Blood

Hot-headed, I may be
Warm-blooded be humankind

Yet I feel an endless flame burn under sun and moon
My skin, fire itself
Melting all that I touch

It is only by hiding in the arctic that my ears do not steam
It is only by dipping into the tundra that I do not exhale ash

Still, I cannot stop the burning
Not when my world is on fire too

Magmar, Secret 10 of 16
