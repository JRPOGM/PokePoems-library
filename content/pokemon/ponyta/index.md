# The Horse with the Diamond Hooves

<blockquote>
A horse strides into town,<br>
Golden mane flowing in the wind,<br>
Copper hide glistening in the daylight,<br>
Diamond hooves clicked on cement.<br>
“I am the greatest stallion in the world,” he declares.<br>
“I can leap anything in a single bound.<br>
If you do not believe I can,<br>
You will pay me in hay.”

So the mayor approaches first and boasts,<br>
“You couldn’t leap over City Hall.”<br>
With a run down I Street,<br>
The stallion clears the government building with a single leap,<br>
And in his landing tramples through beds of flowers.<br>
He gallops up to the mayor and demands,<br>
“Now you will pay me in hay.”

So a beggar approaches and taunts,<br>
“You couldn’t leap over the moon.”<br>
With a run down Main,<br>
The stallion clear over Luna with a single leap,<br>
And in his landing topples a neighborhood.<br>
He gallops up to the beggar and demands,<br>
“Now you will pay me in hay.”

So a child, stars in her eyes, ran up to the stallion and begs,<br>
“Could you jump over the sun too?”<br>
With a run down the freeway,<br>
The stallion clears Sol in a single leap,<br>
Solar flares licking his diamond hooves,<br>
Setting fire to his muscles and mane.<br>
In his landing, he rockets back into the city, clearing it from the map.<br>
He gallops up to the little girl, her charred body in the dirt, and demands,<br>
“Now pay me in hay.”
</blockquote>

## Ponyta, the Fire Horse Pokémon and the Unique Horn Pokémon

![hand-drawn artwork](/images/ponyta.jpeg)

> “_Less than an hour after new Ponyta are born, their manes and tails ignite into fire as their kin and parentage are known for. It does not take long for the Fire Horse Pokémon to have control over this flame, preventing it from actually burning other surfaces or living creatures at its own discretion. People who earn the trust of a Ponyta and its evolution are able to sit atop their backs without the fire harming them, which helped this species find life alongside human civilization and give rise to its fame in sport racing and Pokémon Contests. Because a Ponyta is also a strong creature for its size. Well-trained Ponyta can run for miles without exhaustion, and the best of them can bound over buildings with a single jump and land on their tough hooves without harming their bodies; it is rumored they can eclipse the height of Prism Tower in Lumiose City of the Kalos region._”

> “_But there is a more elusive breed of this Pokémon. Deep within the Glimmerwood Tangle of Galar live a Psychic-type variant of the Ponyta, whose fiery mane is replaced with lush, pastel hair, and the horns they would have grown as a Rapidash are now sported on the younger Ponyta made of a much tougher substance. This horn is said to possess the healing power to cure illness and even fatal wounds, and have made the Pokémon a victim of poaching in the past. It is believed this chase for them made the Pokémon develop the Psychic ability to read a person’s soul and heart, judging their character to know whether to stay or to run from their presence. It is said that randomly encountering one in the wild can bring good fortune in your near future._”
