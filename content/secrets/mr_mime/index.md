# A Message from the Obtuse Office

<blockquote>
How much of it do you believe,<br>
The farce played at your feet?<br>
At what point do your eyes leave?<br>
When does your attention deplete?<br>
What would have convinced you to stay,<br>
I couldn’t feign to assume.<br>
If I gave a damn then maybe I’d pray,<br>
For you to be the one I still groom.<br>
I put in the effort to convince you.<br>
I got so many of you to comply.<br>
But now you really listen to what I spew,<br>
And now it’s me you decry.<br>
I won’t bother shedding a tear.<br>
I don’t plan on leaving here.
</blockquote>
