# Language Barrier

<blockquote>
Man created signals, not just signs<br>
As means of expression down to even the single gesture

The cardinal tilts of the head convey sentences<br>
Fingers, plural or individual, form words with little effort<br>
The twist of the body in a crowd writes paragraphs to watchful eyes<br>
And those same eyes change sizes to pour out their hearts

Sign language slang<br>
A language anyone can speak
</blockquote>
