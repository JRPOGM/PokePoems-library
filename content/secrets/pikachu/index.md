# The Point of Importance

<blockquote>
Who gives you your importance?

You are one in billions<br>
A name shared with millions<br>
From a family lineage of thousands<br>
Even your face, some will recognize another or a dozen so<br>
It is a lot to be compared to<br>
A lot to be seen from someone else in yourself

But individuality sits deeper than that<br>
For all you know<br>
All you like<br>
All you share<br>
Endless arithmetic<br>
Overlap abound but never stacked<br>
For all in life’s venn diagram of humanity<br>
There is a sliver where nothing else sits but you

There is something you don’t share with everyone<br>
And somethings you share with no one<br>
Not because you’re hiding it<br>
Not because you’re ashamed<br>
It is something you made, that only you could<br>
A conception of compounding perceptions<br>
And out from it pours something inherently you

This is your importance<br>
Not in your place in the overlap<br>
But the outstanding sliver you bring to life
</blockquote>
