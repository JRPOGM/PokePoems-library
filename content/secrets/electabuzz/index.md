# The Job Market of Wild West

<blockquote>
Disillusionment is not unwarranted<br>
When shots are fired and you are where the smoke points<br>
Despite your gun still holstered on your hip

You are not the sheriff or the law<br>
But you are armed, as you are wise to be<br>
Prepared to one day stare down the barrel too

Only now, the townsfolk fear their sheriff<br>
Man and woman expecting to be shot in the back<br>
The moment they dare to step off the office porch

It is no wonder tumbleweeds are outgrowing the population<br>
The weed’s responsibilities never change<br>
Nor does the dirt nor the wind threaten their very being

It is expected to feel estranged<br>
When the railway comes into town<br>
And the train makes a mad dash with the money
</blockquote>
