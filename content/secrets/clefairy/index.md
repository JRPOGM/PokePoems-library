# Dancing in the Moonlight

<blockquote>
I believe she is watching, way up high<br>
That where her light shines when nothing brighter is seen

I slide around on dew-kissed hills<br>
Swing in and out between silver-shaded trees<br>
My skin sparkling in her light, no brighter than the stars above her<br>
No attempt to outshine her beauty

I’m out there for her<br>
To remind her she is not alone<br>
That me and many more remain by her side and her sight<br>
Because I am sure she is watching<br>
Afraid, I think, to blink<br>
As if it will erase us all

So I greet her in the mornings when she shows<br>
Perform in the nights when he stares<br>
Believing she is watching me, as I remember her<br>
So when I am gone, she would remember, too<br>
The microorganisms that danced in her eyes
</blockquote>
