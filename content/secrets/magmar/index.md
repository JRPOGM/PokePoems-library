# Warm Blood

<blockquote>
Hot-headed, I may be<br>
Warm-blooded be humankind

Yet I feel an endless flame burn under sun and moon<br>
My skin, fire itself<br>
Melting all that I touch

It is only by hiding in the arctic that my ears do not steam<br>
It is only by dipping into the tundra that I do not exhale ash

Still, I cannot stop the burning<br>
Not when my world is on fire too 
</blockquote>
